# Sandwich - Ham & Cheese

A sandwich with ham and a slice of cheese

NOTE: 150g may be an overestimate. TODO: check with scales.

### Ingredients

 * 2 slices bread
 * 150g ham
 * 1 slice tasty cheese
 * margarine

### Directions

Butter 2 slices of bread and add ham and add slice of cheese.

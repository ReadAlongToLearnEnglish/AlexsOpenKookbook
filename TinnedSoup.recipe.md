# Tinned Soup

A can of tinned soup (one of various flavours, chunky chicken, chunky beef, chicken and corn)


### Ingredients

 * 400 g tin of soup
 * pepper

### Directions

Heat soup in microwave (1 min 30 on medium high) or on stove top

Add pepper to taste.

### Metadata

275 calories / tin (approximate average)
